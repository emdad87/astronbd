About Us


ASTRON Group is a conglomerate of ASTRON Corporation, ASTRON Healthcare, 4S Unani Labs, ASTRON Herbal Toiletries and ASTRON Food and Beverages.

ASTRON Healthcare is a dynamic Marketing & Distribution organization and ASTRON Corporation is an export ,import organization in Bangladesh. ASTRON believes in starting a revolution in healthcare sector, enabling every individual to gain access and benefit from our various Healthcare , Nutraceutical & Pharmaceutical products despite adhering to the highest international standards remain within easy reach of every individual in terms of price and availability.

The organization is managed by dynamic veteran professionals under guidance of A.K.M SIRAJUL HAQUE Managing Director & CEO with zeal and commitment to customer satisfaction and a pragmatic approach to marketing. ASTRON has intimate relations with all their business principals and have carved a niche for themselves by practicing business with sound ethics and prudence.

These days we are marketing high quality imported products. These products are manumactured with high quality ingredients from different sources of USA, Canada, China, Chilli, Thailand etc. We have attained enormous success and expertise to source best quality products from internationally recognized manufacturers. We are also organizing our expertise to collect and export different products from Bangladesh.

Our Nutraceutical products and Herbal Medicine manufacturing plant 4-S Unani Laboratories and Herbal cosmetics manufacturing plant ASTRON Herbal Toiletries are producing "Biotech Herbal" Cosmetics (BSTI Approved). We have already started to produce Nutraceutical / Herbal Medicine products and Herbal cosmetics products from these plant and started to market these products throughout BANGLADESH.

Ours is a team of experts committed to adding the value of professional services to a variety of products and expediting the process of international trade. ASTRON owes its success to a well-trained and dedicated team of professionals having extensive experience in a wide range of Pharmaceutical & Neutraceutical products. The employees are given extensive training and update their knowledge base, to keep abreast of the changing business environment in Bangladesh.

The confidence that ASTRON has in its abilities, the determination to move ahead in the face of odds, an optimistic outlook and a never sagging urge to improve on its performance, have all played a part in helping the organization achieve the status that it enjoys today.
